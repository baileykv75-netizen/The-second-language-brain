# Sandbox evolution

Meaning:
A simulation where life develops freely under simple rules.

Pronunciation:
/SANDBOKS ev-uh-LOO-shuhn/

Example:
The project is a sandbox evolution game rather than a task-based strategy game.

IELTS usage:
Useful for describing games, simulations, technology projects, and creative ideas.

Related words:
simulation, emergence, adaptation
