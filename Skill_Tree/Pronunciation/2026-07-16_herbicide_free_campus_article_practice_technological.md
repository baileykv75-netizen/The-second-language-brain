# Technological

Pronunciation:
/ˌtek.nəˈlɒdʒ.ɪ.kəl/

Common mistake:
Dropping the first syllables and saying “logical development” instead of “technological development”.

Practice sentence:
Technological development can come at a cost if environmental risks are ignored.
