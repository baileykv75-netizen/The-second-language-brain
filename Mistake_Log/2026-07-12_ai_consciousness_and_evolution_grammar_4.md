# Grammar Upgrade 4

Original:
We don't clear where consciousness comes from.

Better:
We still don't fully understand where consciousness comes from.

Explanation:
英语中不能用 “we don't clear” 表示“不清楚”；用 “do not fully understand” 表达知识仍不完整更自然。
