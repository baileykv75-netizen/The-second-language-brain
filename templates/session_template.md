## 2. New Vocabulary

### Spawn

Meaning:
Generate something in a game world.

Pronunciation:
/spɔːn/

Example:
Players can spawn different creatures in the ecosystem.

IELTS usage:
Useful for describing games, technology, simulations, and systems.

Related words:
generate, create, appear

## 3. Grammar Upgrade

Original:
The winner can get the resource and survive.

Better:
The creatures that obtain enough resources are more likely to survive.

Explanation:
使用 relative clause 和更自然的 academic expression，让句子更接近 Band 7.5+ 的表达。

## 5. Personal Expression Bank

### Two-sided system view

Expression:
Everything has two sides, but they should not always be judged as simply good or bad. They are parts of a complete system.

Used for:
Culture, Philosophy, Society, Opinion questions

## 6. Personal Stories

### Zhejiang travel experience

Story:
During my trip to Zhejiang, I realized that a single travel experience can be used to describe a person, a place, an unexpected event, and a personal change.

Used for:
Travel, Places, People, Unexpected Experience
