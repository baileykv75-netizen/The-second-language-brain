# Session Topic

AI Game Development

## 1. Band 7.5-8.0 Model Answer

I would like to design a game based on a living ecosystem. In this game, different creatures can spawn in a shared environment, and each creature needs to obtain resources in order to survive. Some of them may be herbivores that depend on plants, while others may develop different survival strategies over time.

What interests me most is that the game would not simply have a fixed winner. Instead, the creatures that adapt best to the changing environment would be more likely to thrive. This makes the game feel more natural and meaningful, because it reflects how systems work in real life.

I think this kind of game could also help players understand that everything has two sides. A creature that seems weak in one environment may become powerful in another. So the goal is not just to defeat others, but to understand relationships, resources, and long-term balance.

## 2. New Vocabulary

### Spawn

Meaning:
Generate something in a game world.

Pronunciation:
/spɔːn/

Example:
Players can spawn different creatures in the ecosystem.

IELTS usage:
Useful for describing games, technology, simulations, and artificial systems.

Related words:
generate, create, appear

### Herbivore

Meaning:
An animal that eats plants.

Pronunciation:
/ˈhɜːrbɪvɔːr/

Example:
The first creature is a simple herbivore that survives by eating grass.

IELTS usage:
Useful for describing animals, ecosystems, biology, and the environment.

Related words:
ecosystem, plant-eating animal, food chain

### Adapt

Meaning:
Change successfully according to a new situation.

Pronunciation:
/əˈdæpt/

Example:
The creatures that adapt best will survive.

IELTS usage:
Useful for education, technology, work, culture, and environmental questions.

Related words:
adjust, evolve, respond

### Thrive

Meaning:
Grow successfully and become strong.

Pronunciation:
/θraɪv/

Example:
The creatures that adapt best thrive in the ecosystem.

IELTS usage:
Useful for describing people, communities, businesses, and natural systems.

Related words:
flourish, succeed, develop

## 3. Grammar Upgrade

Original:
The winner can get the resource and survive.

Better:
The creatures that obtain enough resources are more likely to survive.

Explanation:
使用 relative clause 表达更清楚的逻辑关系；用 "obtain enough resources" 替代 "get the resource"，表达更准确；用 "are more likely to" 让语气更自然。
