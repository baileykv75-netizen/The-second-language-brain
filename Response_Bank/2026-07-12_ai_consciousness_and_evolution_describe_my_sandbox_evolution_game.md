# Describe my sandbox evolution game

Response:
I'm developing a sandbox evolution game where simple creatures evolve under different environmental conditions. The game begins with bacteria, and the creatures compete for resources, adapt to survival pressure, and gradually follow different evolutionary paths. Rather than completing fixed tasks, players observe how life changes over time.
