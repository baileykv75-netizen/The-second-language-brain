# My Sandbox Evolution Game

Story:
I am building a sandbox evolution simulation that begins with bacteria. Creatures compete for resources, and different environments lead to different evolutionary paths. The player observes the system instead of controlling every detail. The project reflects my belief that freedom creates possibilities and that life does not need a fixed destination to have meaning.
