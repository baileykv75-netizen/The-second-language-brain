# Parameters

Meaning:
The rules, limits, or conditions that shape a system or simulation.

Pronunciation:
/puh-RAM-i-terz/

Example:
Players can adjust the environmental parameters and observe how life adapts.

IELTS usage:
Useful for describing experiments, technology, research methods, and systems.
