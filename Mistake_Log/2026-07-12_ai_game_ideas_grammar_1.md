# Grammar Upgrade 1

Original:
The winner can get the resource and survive.

Better:
The creatures that obtain enough resources are more likely to survive.

Explanation:
使用 relative clause 表达更清楚的逻辑关系；用 "obtain enough resources" 替代 "get the resource"，表达更准确；用 "are more likely to" 让语气更自然。
