# Discuss AI consciousness

Response:
At the moment, I see AI as a tool created by humans. However, if it ever developed genuine consciousness, I think the ethical situation would change. A conscious AI should be respected as another form of life and should coexist peacefully with humans. In my view, rights should be connected with consciousness rather than biological identity alone.
